# Welcome to SLUBI

Welcome to SLUBI, where our mission is to provide support and training in bioinformatics for researchers, students, and faculty at SLU.

As a hub for bioinformatics expertise within the university, we facilitate the application of computational methods in biological research and strieve to build a strong community of bioinformaticians within SLU.

Our staff is located on the 3 SLU campuses in Ultuna, Umeå and Alnarp and available to you in [many different ways](contact.md).
