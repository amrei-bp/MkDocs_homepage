Our bioinformatics courses provide training in a variety of methods for analyzing biological data, covering topics such as sequence analysis, population genetics and reproducible research.

Designed for both beginners and seasoned researchers, our courses blend theoretical foundations with practical applications, training participants in the skills needed to tackle complex biological questions.

We also arrange private courses. If you are interested in a topic you can't find in our list, please [contact us](contact.qmd).
